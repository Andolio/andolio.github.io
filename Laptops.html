Remember the following:

  • Minimum Requirements
    • 4GB of RAM
    • 256GB of Storage
    • Windows 7 or 8
    • Intel Core i5 processor or AMD processor
    • 1GB Graphics Card
  • Recommended Requirements
    • 8GB of RAM
    • Memory:
      1. 500GB or 1TB of HDD Storage.
      2. 256GB SSD Storage
    • Windows 8 64bit or Windows 10 64bit.
    • Processor
      1. Intel Core i7 processor
      2. AMD Quad-Core
      3. AMD 6-Core
      4. AMD 8-Core
    • Graphics Card:
      1. 2GB Graphics Card
      2. 4GB Graphics Card
      3. Any Other Graphics Card Higher than 4GB it's your decision
